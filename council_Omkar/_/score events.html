Events

Football

Halftime is 20 minutes, with 5 minutes between halves. All standard FIFA rules are applicable. All teams are requested to give the complete list of squad when they report for their matches, along with their college identity cards. Referees cannot be questioned and the decision will be final.

Rink football

Rolling substitutes are allowed. Halftime is 10 minutes with 5 minutes break between halves. The draws are made by the committee and cannot be changed or questioned. Referees cannot be questioned and the decision will be final. Other rules will be told before the match.

Rink football (Girls)

Rolling substitutes are allowed. Halftime is 10 minutes with 5 minutes break between halves. The draws are made by the committee and cannot be changed or questioned. Referees cannot be questioned and the decision will be final. Other rules will be told before the match.

Table Tennis

Each game will be a set of ‘best of 3’. The players are required to switch the sides after each match. Each match will be of 11 points. The referee’s decision will be final and cannot be questioned. Standard rules of TT applicable.

Badminton

Tournament progresses as knockout rounds. All the matches will be a game of ‘best of 3’ set and each match will have 21 points. Standard badminton rules applicable throughout the game.

Box cricket

Team of 6+2 is required to play the game. 1 girl compulsory is the team, else you’re disqualified. Standard Box rules applicable. The Umpire's decision is final and cannot be questioned.

carrom

It is an individual event. Adjusting the chairs after the match starts will not be allowed. Touching of coins while picking the striker will be considered a foul. Universal rules of carrom apply throughout the game.

chess

Draws will be declared by the committee and cannot be questioned. Each player will be provided with 45 minutes of play. In case of draw a Blitz, match of 5 minutes will decide the winner. Participants are requested to bring their own chessboard.

volleyball

There will be a ‘best of 3’ game set. The first and second match will be of 25 points and the third of 15. Touching the net will be considered a foul and a point will be given to the opponent team. Crossing the line under the net and the line of service will also be considered a foul

throwball

Show off your quick reflexes catching and throwing the ball around with your amazing team!
